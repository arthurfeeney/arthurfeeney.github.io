Title: About

In Fall 2022, I will be beginning a PhD. in Computer Engineering at UC Irvine.
I am broadly interested in High Performance Computing (HPC) and software systems,
especially as applied to scientific problems.

I received an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Massachussetts Amherst
and a B.S. from Trinity University. Before starting my PhD., I worked as an SDE at Amazon.
While at UMass Amherst, I had the great fortune to do internships with the Numerical Algorithms
Group (NAG) and Amazon, as well as a part-time academic mentorship with two researchers at
IBM Research.
