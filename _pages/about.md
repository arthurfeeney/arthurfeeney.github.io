I am a new PhD candidate in the department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of California Irvine. I am a member of the HPC Forge and am advised by Dr. Aparna Chandramowlishwaran. I am interested in high performance computing and software systems, particularly when used to accelarate scientific research.

I recieved an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Massachussetts Amherst and a B.S. with Honors in Computer Science from Trinity University. Prior to joining UC Irvine, I worked as a software developer at Amazon. I have also had the great fortune to intern with the Numerical Algorithms Group (NAG) and Amazon, as well as do an part-time academic mentorship with researchers from IBM Research.
